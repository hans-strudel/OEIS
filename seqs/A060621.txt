%N A060621 Number of flips between the d-dimensional tilings of the unary zonotope Z(D,d). Here the codimension D-d is equal to 3 and d varies.
%D A060621 A. Bjorner, M. Las Vergnas, B. Sturmfels, N. White and G. M. Ziegler, Oriented Matroids, Encyclopedia of Mathematics 46, Second Edition, Cambridge University Press, 1999.
%D A060621 N. Destainville, R. Mosseri and F. Bailly, Fixed-boundary octagonal random tilings: a combinatorial approach, Journal of Statistical Physics, 102 (2001), no. 1-2, 147-190.

%F A060621 Empirical g.f.: -4*(7*x^2-9*x+3) / (2*x-1)^3. - _Colin Barker_, Feb 20 2013
%e A060621 For any Z(d,d), there is a unique tiling therefore the first term of the series is 0. Likewise, there are always two tilings of Z(d+1,d) with a flip between them, therefore the second term of the series is 1.
