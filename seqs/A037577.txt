%F A037577 a(n) = (5^n - 2)/3 for n odd ; a(n) = (5^n - 1)/3 for n even. - _Ctibor O. Zizka_, Apr 15 2008
%F A037577 a(n) = floor(5^n/3). - _Gary Detlefs_, Sep 06 2010
