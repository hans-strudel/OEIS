%N A059804 Consider the line segment in R^n from the origin to the point v=(2,3,5,7,11,...) with prime coordinates; let d = squared distance to this line from the closest point of Z^n (excluding the endpoints). Sequence gives d times v.v.
%C A059804 v.v is given by A024450(n). For n >= 19, a(n) = A024450(n-1).
%C A059804 Officially these are just conjectures so far.
