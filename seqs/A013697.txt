%F A013697 a(n) = 2^n - (4/3)^n + O(1). It appears that a(n) = 2^n - floor((4/3)^n) - k, where k is usually 2 but is sometimes 1. Up to n=1000, the only values of n where k = 1 are 4, 5, 13, 14, and 17. (End)
