%N A034923 Numbers n such that 43^n-42 is prime.
%C A034923 Related to hyperperfect numbers of a certain form.
%C A034923 The 1017 term produces a 1662-digit number which is only a probable prime. Any additional terms are greater than 1500.
%C A034923 The next terms are > 3000. - _Vincenzo Librandi_, Sep 27 2012
