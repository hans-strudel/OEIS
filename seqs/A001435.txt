%N A001435 Number of connected graphs with n nodes and n+1 edges.
%C A001435 This enumerates the connected graphs of complexity 2, in the terminology of Spencer, p.720. We define the complexity of a component [of the random graph] with V vertices and E edges as E-V+1. Trees [A000055] and unicyclic graphs [A001429] have complexity 0 and 1, respectively, and are called simple. A diagonal of A076263. [From _Jonathan Vos Post_, Jun 26 2010]
