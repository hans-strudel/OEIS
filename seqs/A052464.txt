%N A052464 Fixed points for operation of repeatedly replacing a number by the sum of the fifth power of its digits.
%C A052464 Equivalently, numbers equal to the sum of 5-th powers of their decimal digits. Since this sum is <= 9^5*d for a d-digit number n >= 10^(d-1), there cannot be such a number with more than 6 digits. - _M. F. Hasler_, Apr 12 2015
