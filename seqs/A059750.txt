%F A059750 The previous result is the case q=2 of the following generalization:
%F A059750 Zeta(1/q) = lim_{k->inf} (Sum_{n=1..k} 1/n^(1/q) - (q/(q-1))*k^((q-1)/q)), with q>1. Example: for q=3/2, Zeta(2/3) = lim_{k->inf} (Sum_{n=1..k} 1/n^(2/3) - 3*k^(1/3)) = -2.447580736233658231... (End)
