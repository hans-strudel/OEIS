%N A057712 Number of "polyhes" of order n: a polyhe of order n is obtained by taking a polyhex made of n hexagons (A000228); cutting each of the n hexagons along a diameter and throwing away half that hexagon, in such a way that the remaining figure (made of n half-hexagons) is connected.
