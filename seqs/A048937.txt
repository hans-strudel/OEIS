%N A048937 Numbers n with an even number of digits, n = d_1 d_2 ... d_n, such that there are exactly three ways to partition the digits into two groups of size n/2, say f_1 ... f_{n/2} and g_1 ... g_{n/2}, such that n = f_1 ... f_{n/2} * g_1 ... g_{n/2}.
%C A048937 f_{n/2} and g_{n/2} may not both be zero.
%C A048937 Vampire numbers (definition 2) having exactly three distinct pairs of fangs.
