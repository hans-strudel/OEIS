%F A006253 From Dan Fux (dan.fux(AT)OpenGaia.com or danfux(AT)OpenGaia.com), Apr 11 2001: The values are a(1) = 2 * 1^2, a(2) = 3^2, a(3) = 2 * 4^2, a(4) = 11^2, a(5) = 2 * 15^2, ... and in general for odd n a(n) is twice a square, for even n a(n) is a square. If we define b(n) by b(n) = sqrt(a(n)) for even n, b(n) = sqrt(a(n)/2) for odd n then apart from the first 2 elements b(n) is A002530(n+1).
