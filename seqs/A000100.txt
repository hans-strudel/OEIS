%N A000100 a(n) = number of compositions of n in which the maximal part is 3.
%C A000100 For n > 5, a(n) - (a(n-3)+a(n-2)+a(n-1)) = F(n-2) where F(i) is the i-th Fibonacci number; e.g., 11 - (1+2+5) = 3, 23 - (2+5+11) = 8; also lim_{n->inf} a(n)/(a(n-1)+a(n-2)+a(n-3)) = 1 and lim_{n->inf} a(n)a(n-2)/a(n-1)^2 = 1. - _Gerald McGarvey_, Jun 26 2004
%C A000100 a(n) is also the number of binary sequences of length n-1 in which the longest run of 0's is exactly two. - _Geoffrey Critzer_, Nov 06 2008
