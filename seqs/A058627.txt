%N A058627 Numbers n such that phi(n) = product of the digits of n.
%C A058627 Sequence is finite because for n large enough phi(n) > n / (exp(EulerGamma) * log(log(n))+3/log(log(n))), while pod(n) < 9*9^ceiling(log(n)/log(10)). If a(16) exists, it holds 10^13 < a(16) < 10^64. - _Giovanni Resta_, Apr 22 2014
