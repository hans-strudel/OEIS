%N A000176 Generalized tangent numbers d_(n,2).
%C A000176 Consider the Dirichlet series L_a(s) = sum_{k>=0)} (-a|2k+1) / (2k+1)^s, where (-a|2k+1) is the Jacobi symbol. Then the numbers d_(a,n) are defined by L_a(2n)= (Pi/(2a))^(2n)*sqrt(a)* d_(a,n)/ (2n-1)! for a>1 and n=1,2,3...
