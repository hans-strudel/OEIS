%N A038810 a(n) is the number of ways to write the number n as dk...di...d2d1d0 where digit di represents di * (di+1)^i, restricting di to 0..9.
%e A038810 a(7)=5 since 7 = (7*8^0) = (1*2^1)+(5*6^0) = (2*3^1)+(1*2^0) = (1*2^2)+(0*1^1)+(3*4^0) = (1*2^2)+(1*2^1)+(1*2^0), which are the numbers {7,15,21,103,111}.
