%N A001041 a(0)=12; thereafter a(n) = 12 times the product of the first n primes.
%C A001041 Bob Marshall calls these the "holotomic numbers" and opines: "This sequence, aptly designated as the Holotomic Sequence, represents the minimal plural quantities that accomodate the maximum amount of consecutive factors." Thus this can be seen as a version of A051451. - _Charles R Greathouse IV_, Jan 12 2012
%C A001041 If we were to take the maximum number of consecutive factors as the definition, the sequence would be prefixed with 1, 2, 6. - _Franklin T. Adams-Watters_, Jun 13 2015
