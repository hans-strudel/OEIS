%N A003110 a(n) = number of special odd permutations of 2*n+1.
%C A003110 a(n) is the number of odd permutations, pi(0),...,pi(2*n), of 0,...,2*n such that pi(0) = 0 and p(k) = k + pi(k) (mod 2*n+1), k=0,...,2*n is also a permutation of 0,...,2*n. - _Sean A. Irvine_, Jan 31 2015
