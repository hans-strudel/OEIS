%N A054273 Number of primes p in the interval prime(n+1) <= p < prime(n+1)^2 such that A002110(n)+p is prime.
%e A054273 n=3, prime(4)=7, prime(4)^2=49; 3rd primorial number = 30; in interval [7,49] 12 primes p occur of which 10 are such that 30+p is prime, namely 30+{7,11,13,17,23,29,31,37,41,43} = {37,41,...,73}, "post-primorial primes", while two primes 19 and 47 yield 49, 77 which are composites. So a(3)=10.
