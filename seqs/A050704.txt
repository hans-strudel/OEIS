%N A050704 Composite numbers with property that n minus sum of prime factors of n is prime.
%C A050704 Prime factors are totaled with multiplicity, e.g., 8 = 2*2*2 so the sum of the prime factors of 8 = 6. - _Harvey P. Dale_, Jun 14 2011
%e A050704 E.g., 161 = 7*23; 161 - (7+23) = 161 - 30 = 131, which is prime.
