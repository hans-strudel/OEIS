%N A003601 Numbers n such that the average of the divisors of n is an integer: sigma_0(n) divides sigma_1(n). Alternatively, tau(n) (A000005(n)) divides sigma(n) (A000203(n)).
%C A003601 Sometimes called arithmetic numbers.
%C A003601 Generalized (sigma_r)-numbers are numbers n for which sigma_r(n)/sigma_0(n) = c^r. Sigma_r(n) denotes sum of r-th powers of divisors of n; c,r positive integers. The numbers in this sequence are sigma_1-numbers; those in A140480 are sigma_2-numbers. - _Ctibor O. Zizka_, Jul 14 2008
