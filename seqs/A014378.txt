%N A014378 Number of connected regular graphs of degree 8 with n nodes.
%C A014378 Since the nontrivial 8-regular graph with the least number of vertices is K_9, there are no disconnected 8-regular graphs with less than 18 vertices. Thus for n<18 this sequence is identical to A180260. - _Jason Kimberley_, Sep 25 2009 and Feb 10 2011
