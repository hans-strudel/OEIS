%N A007783 Mixed Van der Waerden numbers w(n, 3; 2).
%C A007783 This is the smallest number M such that if each integer 1, 2, ..., M is colored using one of two colors (say red and blue), then there must be an arithmetic progression of length 3 in one color (red) or an arithmetic progression of length n in the other color (blue). So the first term, w(1, 3; 2), is 3. - _Donald Vestal_, May 31 2005
