%N A026026 a(n) = number of (s(0), s(1), ..., s(2n-1)) such that s(i) is a nonnegative integer and |s(i) - s(i-1)| = 1 for i = 1,2,...,n, s(0) = 3, s(2n-1) = 4. Also a(n) = T(2n-1,n-1), where T is defined in A026022.
