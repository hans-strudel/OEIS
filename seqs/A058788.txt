%N A058788 Triangle T(n,k) = number of polyhedra (triconnected planar graphs) with n edges and k vertices (or k faces), where (n/3+2) <= k <= (2n/3). Note that there is no such k when n=7.
%C A058788 Rows are of lengths 1,0,1,2,1,2,3,2,3,4,3,4,5,4,5,6,5, ... n-1-2*floor((n+2)/3). See A008611. Note the zero length, which means that there are no polyhedra with n=7 edges.
%H A058788 G. P. Michon, <a href="http://www.numericana.com/data/polyhedra.htm">Counting Polyhedra</a>
%e A058788 There are 768 different polyhedra with 18 edges and 9 or 11 faces.
