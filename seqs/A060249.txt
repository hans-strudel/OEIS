%N A060249 Size of the automorphism group of the symmetric group S_n.
%C A060249 For all n except n=2 and n=6, Aut(S_n) is S_n itself, i.e. S_n has no outer automorphisms. Aut(S_2) is trivial and Aut(S_6) is of order 2*|S_6| = 1440 - there is an outer involution.
%F A060249 a(n) = n! except for n=2 and 6.
