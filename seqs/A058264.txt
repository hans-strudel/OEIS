%N A058264 Smallest prime p of two consecutive primes, p < q, such that GCD( p-1, q-1 ) = 2n.
%C A058264 Since all consecutive primes, p < q and p greater than 2, are odd, therefore GCD( p-1, q-1 ) must be even.
