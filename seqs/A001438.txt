%N A001438 Maximal number of mutually orthogonal Latin squares (or MOLS) of order n.
%C A001438 By convention, a(0) = a(1) = infinity.
%C A001438 Parker and others conjecture that a(10) = 2.
%C A001438 It is also known that a(11) = 10, a(12) >= 5.
%C A001438 a(n) <= n-1 for all n>1. - _Tom Edgar_, Apr 27 2015
%C A001438 a(p^k) = p^k-1 for all primes p and k>0. - _Tom Edgar_, Apr 27 2015
