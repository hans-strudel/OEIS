%N A056697 a(n) is least N > 1 congruent to -1,0, or 1 mod i for all i=1,...,n.

%F A056697 Since n!-1 == -1 mod for all i = 1..n, a(n) <= n!-1 for n>2. - _N. J. A. Sloane_, Dec 30 2012
%e A056697 a(9) = 55 because 55 gives remainder -1 when divided by 2,4,7 and 8, gives remainder 0 when divided by (1 and) 5, and gives remainder 1 when divided by 3,6 and 9. All smaller integers greater than 1 give remainders other than -1, 0, or 1 for at least one of 5,6,7,8, or 9.
