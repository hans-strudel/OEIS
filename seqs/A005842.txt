%N A005842 a(n) = minimal integer m such that an m X m square contains non-overlapping squares of sides 1, ..., n (some values are only conjectures).
%C A005842 The entries for n=1, 2, 8, 15, 16, 17, 19, 20, 21, 22, 23, 25, 27, 29, 30, 31, 35, 36, 37, 39, 41, 43, 44, 45, 46, 49, 50, 51, 54, and 56 all meet the lower bound in A092137 and are therefore correct. - _Stuart E Anderson_, Jan 05 2008
%C A005842 Simonis, H. and O'Sullivan showed that a(26) = 80. - _Erich Friedman_, May 27 2009
%C A005842 Houhardy S. showed a(32)=108, a(33)=113, a(34)=118, and a(47)=190. - _Erich Friedman_, Oct 11 2010
%C A005842 The values have been proved correct except those for n=28, 38, 40, 42, 48, 52, 53 and 55, where they remain probable. - _Erich Friedman_, Oct 11 2010
