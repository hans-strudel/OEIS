%C A001609 This comment covers a family of sequences which satisfy a recurrence of the form a(n) = a(n-1) + a(n-m), with a(n) = 1 for n = 1...m-1, a(m) = m+1. The generating function is (x+m*x^m)/(1-x-x^m). Also a(n) = 1 + n*sum(binomial(n-1-(m-1)*i, i-1)/i, i=1..n/m). This gives the number of ways to cover (without overlapping) a ring lattice (or necklace) of n sites with molecules that are m sites wide. Special cases: m=2: A000204, m=3: A001609, m=4: A014097, m=5: A058368, m=6: A058367, m=7: A058366, m=8: A058365, m=9: A058364.
%C A001609 The sequence defined by a(n)-1 plays a role for the computation of A065414, A146486, A146487, and A146488 equivalent to the role of A001610 for A005596, A146482, A146483 and A146484, see the variable a_{2,n} in arXiv:0903.2514. - _R. J. Mathar_, Mar 28 2009
