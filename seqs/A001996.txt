%C A001996 Also, Molien series for invariants of finite Coxeter group A_6. The Molien series for the finite Coxeter group of type A_k (k >= 1) has G.f. = 1/Prod_{i=2..k+1} (1-x^i). - _N. J. A. Sloane_, Jan 11 2016
