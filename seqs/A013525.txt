%N A013525 E.g.f.: x + (gdinv x - sinh x)/2, where gdinv = inverse-Gudermannian. Sequence has odd-indexed coefficients; others are zero.
%C A013525 Also gdinv(x) = log(tan(x)+sec(x)).
%F A013525 For n>0, a(n) = (1/2) * (A000364(n) - 1).
