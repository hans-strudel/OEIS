%N A001577 An operational recurrence.
%C A001577 Considers recurrences u(n+1) = (d/dx) u_n(x)*u_{n-1}(x) and u(n+1) = x*(d/dx) u_n(x)*u_{n-1}(x). In latter, take u_0=1, u_1=x; setting x=1 gives sequence shown here.
