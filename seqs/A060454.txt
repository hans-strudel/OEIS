%N A060454 Consider the line segment in R^n from the origin to the point v = (1,4,9,...,n^2); let d = squared distance to this line from the closest point of Z^n (excluding the endpoints). Sequence gives d times v.v.
%C A060454 v.v is given by A000538(n).
%C A060454 Officially these are just conjectures so far.
