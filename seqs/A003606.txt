%F A003606 G.f.: Product_{k >= 1} f(x^k)^p_k, where f(x)=Product_{k >= 0} 1/(1-x^k) = Sum_{k >= 0} p_k*x^k and p_k is the number of partitions of k (A000041).
%F A003606 Recurrence relation: a(n+1) = 1/(n+1) * Sum_{k=0..n} a(k)*g(n-k+1) where g(n) = Sum_{ij | n} p(i)*i*j, with the sum over all ordered pairs (i, j) such that their products divide n and p(i) is the number of partitions of i. Also a(0)=1.
