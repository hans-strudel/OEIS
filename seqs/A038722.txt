%C A038722 . A reordering of the natural numbers.
%C A038722 . The sequence is self-inverse in that a(a(n)) = n.
%C A038722 . Also: a(1) = 1, a(n) = m (where m is the least triangular number > a(k) for 1 <= k < n), if the minimal natural number not encountered so far is greater than a(n-1), else a(n) = a(n-1)-1. (End)
