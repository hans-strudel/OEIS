%N A034592 Cycle of 2 steps possible for 'concatenate a(n) and nextprime(a(n)) is a prime'.
%e A034592 E.g. a(n)=777 -> nextprime(a(n)) is 787 so '777787' is prime (=step 1); a(n2)=777787 -> nextprime(a(n2)) is 777817 so '777787777817' is prime (=step 2).
