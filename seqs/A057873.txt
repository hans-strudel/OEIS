%N A057873 a(1) = 1; a(n+1) = sum of terms in continued fraction for sum{k=1 to n}[a(n+1-k)/a(k)].
%e A057873 Sum{k=1 to 4}[a(5-k)/a(k)] = 5/1 +2/1 +1/2 +1/5 = 77/10 =7 +1/(1 +1/(2 +1/3)). So a(5) = 7 +1 +2 +3 = 13.
