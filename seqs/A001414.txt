%N A001414 Integer log of n: sum of primes dividing n (with repetition).
%C A001414 MacMahon calls this the potency of n.
%C A001414 Sometimes also called sopfr(n).
%C A001414 Downgrades the operators in a prime decomposition. E.g., 40 factors as 2^3 * 5 and sopfr(40) = 2 * 3 + 5 = 11.
%C A001414 Consider all ways of writing n as a product of zero, one, or more factors; sequence gives smallest sum of terms. - _Amarnath Murthy_, Jul 07 2001
%C A001414 a(n) <= n for all n, and a(n) = n iff n is 4 or a prime.
