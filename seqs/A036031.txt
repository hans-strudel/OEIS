%N A036031 Number of partitions of n into parts not of form 4k+2, 24k, 24k+3 or 24k-3. Also number of partitions in which no odd part is repeated, with at most 2 parts of size less than or equal to 2 and where differences between parts at distance 5 are greater than 1 when the smallest part is odd and greater than 2 when the smallest part is even.
