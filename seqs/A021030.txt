%N A021030 Decimal expansion of 1/26.
%C A021030 A tool code breakers sometimes use is the index of coincidence, I_c. According to Swenson (2008), "the theoretically perfect I_c is if all characters occur the exact same number of times so that none was more likely than any other to be repeated." For cyphertext encrypted from English text (using an alphabet of 26 letters) of infinite length, this means there exists the infinite limit (n - 1)/(26n - 1) which by L'Hopital's rule works out to 1/26. - _Alonso del Arte_, Sep 13 2011
