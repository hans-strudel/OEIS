%N A001676 Number of h-cobordism classes of smooth homotopy n-spheres.
%C A001676 For n not equal to 4 (and possibly for all n) this is the number of oriented diffeomorphism classes of differentiable structures on the n-sphere.
%C A001676 a(3) = 1 follows now that the Poincar√© conjecture has been proved.
%C A001676 a(n) for n != 4 is the order of S_n, the n-th group in Tables 1 and 2 (explained in Further Details p. 807) of Milnor 2011.
