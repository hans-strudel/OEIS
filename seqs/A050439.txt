%F A050439 Let C(n) be the n-th composite number, with C(1)=4. Then these are numbers C(C(C(C(C(n))))).
%e A050439 C(C(C(C(C(8))))) = C(C(C(C(15)))) = C(C(C(25))) = C(C(38)) = C(55) = 77. So 77 is in the sequence.
%p A050439 C := remove(isprime,[$4..1000]): seq(C[C[C[C[C[n]]]]],n=1..100);
