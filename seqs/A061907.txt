%N A061907 The iterative cycle: n -> sum of digits of n^2 has only four distinct elements.
%e A061907 a(2) = 4 since 2 -> 4 -> 1+6 = 7 -> 4+9 = 13 -> 1+6+9 = 16 -> 2+5+6 = 13, thus {4,7,13,16} are the distinct elements of the iterative cycle of 2.
