%C A051044 _Eric W. Weisstein_ comments: "The values of n for which a(n) is prime are 3, 4, 5, 7, 22, 70, 100, 495, 1247, 2072, 320397, ... (A035359), with no others for n <= 3015000 (Weisstein, May 06 2000). These values correspond to 2, 2, 3, 5, 89, 29927, 444793, 602644050950309, ... (A051005).
