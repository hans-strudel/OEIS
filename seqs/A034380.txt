%N A034380 Ratio of totient to Carmichael's lambda function: a(n) = A000010(n) / A002322(n).
%C A034380 a(n)=1 if and only if the multiplicative group modulo n is cyclic (that is, if n is either 1, 2, 4, or of the form p^k or 2*p^k where p is an odd prime). In other words: a(n)=1 for n is a term of A033948, otherwise a(n)>1 (and n is a term of A033949). [_Joerg Arndt_, Jul 14 2012]
