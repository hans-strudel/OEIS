%F A008297 We have the following expressions for the Lah polynomial L_n(y)=Sum_{k=0..n} |a(n, k)|*y^k -- exact generalizations of results in A000262 for A000262(n) = L_n(1):
%F A008297 1) L_n(y) = y*exp(-y)*n!*M(n+1,2,y), n>=1, where M (=1F1) is the confluent hypergeometric function of the first kind;
%F A008297 2) L_n(y) = exp(-y)* Sum_{m>=0} y^m*[m]^n/m!, n>=0, where [m]^n = m*(m+1)*...*(m+n-1) is the rising factorial;
%F A008297 3) L_n(y) = (2n-2+y)L_{n-1}(y)-(n-1)(n-2)L_{n-2}(y), n>=2;
%F A008297 4) L_n(y) = y*(n-1)!*Sum_{k=1..n} (L_{n-k}(y) k!)/((n-k)! (k-1)!), n>=1. (End)
