%N A053046 EulerPhi is iterated with initial value n!; a(n) = number of terms that are not powers of 2 among the iterates.
%C A053046 Non-powers of 2 arise at the beginning of iteration chains without interruption. Analogous to A053036.
%e A053046 n=10, initial v.=3628800, the iteration chain is: {3628800,829440,221184,73728,24576, 8192,4096,2048,1024,512,256,128,64,32,16,8,4,2,1}. Its length is 19 and the 5 initial values are not powers of 2: 10!,..,24576. Thus a(10)=5.
