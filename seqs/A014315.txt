%N A014315 Number of permutations of squarefree monomials of degree n in n non-commutative variables that are compatible with a non-commutative monomial order (in the Groebner basis sense).
%e A014315 For example, when n=3, 132 of the (3!)!=720 possible permutations of xyz,xzy,yxz,yzx,zxy,zyx are compatible with a monomial order.
