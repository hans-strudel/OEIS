%N A050647 a(n+1)^3 is next smallest cube ending with a(n)^3, initial term is 1.
%C A050647 Frank Ellermann conjectures (Jun 11 2001) that if b(n) = 10^(3^n -1), i.e., 1, 100, 100000000, etc. then a(n) = concatenation b( n-2 ) || a( n-1 ) for n > 1.
%C A050647 a(6) is too large to include (contains 122 digits).
%F A050647 a(n) = sum_{i=0..n-1} 10^((3^i-1)/2). - _Max Alekseyev_, Jan 07 2015
