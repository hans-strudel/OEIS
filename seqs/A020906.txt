%o A020906 (PARI) at(n)=local(r,k);r=vector(n*(n+1)\2);r[1]=r[3]=1;r[2]=2;k=4;for(i=3,n,for(j=1,i,r[k]=r[i-j+1];k++));r /* Generates first n>1 rows of triangle. - Franklin T. Adams-Watters, Aug 08 2011. */
