%N A056203 Triangle of numbers related to congruum problem: T(n,k)=n^2+2nk-k^2 with n>k>0, starting at T(2,1)=7.
%C A056203 The congruum problem is to find h (A057103) such that there are integers x (A055096), y (A057105) and z (A056203) with h=x^2-y^2=z^2-x^2.
%C A056203 Refers to A057102, which had an incorrect description and has been replaced by A256418. As a result the present sequence should be re-checked. - _N. J. A. Sloane_, Apr 06 2015

%e A056203 a(1) = T(2,1) = 2^2+2*2*1-1 = 7.
