%N A001562 Numbers n such that (10^n + 1)/11 is a prime.
%C A001562 The a(10) to a(11) gap represents the largest relative gap seen so far in searching repunits with bases between -12 and 12. On average, there should have been 4 more primes added to this sequence by a(11), instead of just 1. - _Paul Bourdelais_, Feb 11 2010
