%N A058330 a(n) is smallest positive integer, distinct from any terms earlier in the sequence, such that (sum{k=1 to n}[a(k)]) divides (product{k=1 to n}[a(k)])*(sum{k=1 to n}[1/a(k)]).
%e A058330 a(4) = 3 because 3 is smallest positive integer m, not = to 1, 2, or 4, where (1 + 2 + 4 + m) divides 1 * 2 * 4 * m * (1 + 1/2 + 1/4 + 1/m).
