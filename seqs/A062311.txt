%N A062311 Number of ways writing n! as a sum of two primes.
%C A062311 Number of unordered pairs of primes (p,q) such that n! = p + q.
%F A062311 In other words, a(n) = A061358(n!) = number of prime+prime partitions of n!.
%e A062311 n = 4: 4! = 24 = 5+19 = 7+17 = 11+13 so a(4) = 3.
