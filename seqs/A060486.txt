%N A060486 Tricoverings of an n-set.
%C A060486 A covering of a set is a tricovering if every element of the set is covered by exactly three blocks of the covering.
%F A060486 E.g.f. for k-block tricoverings of an n-set is exp(-x+x^2/2+(exp(y)-1)*x^3/3)*Sum_{k=0..inf}x^k/k!*exp(-1/2*x^2*exp(k*y))*exp(binomial(k, 3)*y).
%e A060486 There are 1 4-block tricovering, 3 5-block tricoverings and 1 6-block tricovering of a 3-set,(cf. A060487), so a(3)=5.
