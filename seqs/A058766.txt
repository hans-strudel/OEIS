%N A058766 a(0) = 1, a(1) = 2; for n>=2 a(n) is the number of degree-n reducible polynomials over GF(2).
%C A058766 Dimensions of homogeneous subspaces of shuffle algebra defined in the "Comments" line.
%C A058766 Let x and y be two letters, m and m' any two words, e is the empty word of the free monoid generated by (x,y). Let uu denote the shuffle or Hurwitz product: xm uu ym' =x.(m uu ym') + y.(xm uu m'); of course, e is neutral.
%D A058766 M. Lothaire, Combinatorics on words, Cambridge mathematical library, 1983, p. 126 (definition of shuffle algebra).
%F A058766 For n>=2, a(n) = 2^n - A001037(n).
%e A058766 Degree 3: x uu x = 2 x^2, y uu y = 2 y^2, x uu y = xy + yx.
