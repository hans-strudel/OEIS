%N A054349 Successive generations of the variant of the Kolakoski sequence described in A042942.
%C A054349 For n>=0, let f_1(n) be the number of 1's in a(n) (sequence begins : 0,0,2,3,4,6,11,17,24,...) and f_2(n) be the number of 2's (sequence begins :1,2,2,3,5,8,11,16,25...). Then there is a simple relation between f_1 and f_2, namely : f_1(n)=1-f_2(n)+f_2(n-1)+f_2(n-2)+...+f_2(1)+f_2(0) . i.e. f_1(7)=17 and 1-f_2(7)+f_2(6)+...+f_2(0)=1-16+11+8+5+3+2+2+1=17 - _Benoit Cloitre_, Oct 11 2005
