%N A001843 The coding-theoretic function A(n,4,4).
%C A001843 Maximal number of 4-subsets of an n-set such that any two subsets meet in at most 2 points.
%C A001843 The initial known values are 1, 1, 3, 7, 14, 18, 30, 35, 51, 65, 91, 105, 140, 157, 198, 228, 285, 315, 385, 418 or 419, 498, 550, 650, 702, 819, 877, 1005, 1085, 1240, 1320, 1496, >= 1576, ...
%C A001843 The first two open cases are A(23,4,4) >= 418 and A(35,4,4) >= 1576.
