%N A061194 Minimum positive value of LCM{1,...,n}*(s_1/1 + ... + s_n/n), where each s_i equals 1 or -1.
%C A061194 Nonnegative by considering the largest power of 2 up to n
%e A061194 1/1 - 1/2 - 1/3 - 1/4 - 1/5 + 1/6 + 1/7 = 11/420 = 11/LCM[1,2,3,4,5,6,7], so a(7)<=11; no other choice of signs yields 1/420, ..., 10/420, so a(7)=11.
