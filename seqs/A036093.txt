%N A036093 Centered cube numbers: (n+1)^15+n^15.
%C A036093 Never prime nor semiprime, nor triprime, as a(n) = (2n+1) * (n^2 + n + 1) * (n^4 + 2n^3 + 4n^2 + 3n + 1) * (n^8 + 4n^7 + 30n^6 + 76n^5 + 99n^4 + 76n^3 + 35n^2 + 9n + 1).  Has the nontrivial minimum 4 prime factors when n is in {1, 5, 105, ...} [Jonathan Vos Post, Aug 27 2011].
