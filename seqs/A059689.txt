%N A059689 List consisting of all functions from [1,2,3,...,n] into [1,2,3,...,n] for n=1,2,3,... (written out in full) in lexicographic order.
%C A059689 Includes every finite sequence of positive integers. - _Franklin T. Adams-Watters_, Jan 26 2006
%e A059689 For n=1 there is one function, 1. For n=2 there are 4 such functions and ordering these lexicographically we get (1,1), (1,2), (2,1), (2,2). Thus the sequence begins 1; (1,1), (1,2), (2,1), (2,2); ...
