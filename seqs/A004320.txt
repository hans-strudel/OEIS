%C A004320 Consider the set B(n) = {1,2,3,...n}. Let a(0) = 0. Then a(n) = Sum [ b(i)^2 - b(j)^2] for all i, j = 1 to n, b(i) belongs to B(n). E.g., a(3) = (3^2-1^2) + (3^2-2^2) + (2^2-1^2) = 16. - _Amarnath Murthy_, Jun 01 2001
