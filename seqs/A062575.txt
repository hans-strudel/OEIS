%N A062575 a(n) = maximum over m of the size of the largest subset of pairwise relatively prime numbers in {m+1, m+2, ..., m+n}.
%H A062575 P. Erd≈ës and J. L. Selfridge, <a href="http://www.renyi.hu/~p_erdos/1971-03.pdf">Complete prime subsets of consecutive integers</a>, Proceedings of the Manitoba Conference on Numerical Mathematics, Winnipeg (1971), p. 13.
%e A062575 a(5) = 4, since {1,2,3,4,5} contains the subset {1,2,3,5} which is pairwise relatively prime and it is impossible for 5 consecutive positive integers to be pairwise relatively prime.
