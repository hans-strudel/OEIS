%F A053630 a(1) = 3, a(n) = (a(n-1)^2 + 1)/2 for n > 1.
%F A053630 a(n) = 2*A000058(n)-1 = A053631(n)+1 = floor[2 * 1.597910218031873...^(2^n)].
