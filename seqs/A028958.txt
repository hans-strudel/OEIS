%N A028958 Numbers represented by quadratic form with Gram matrix [ 2, 1; 1, 12 ] (divided by 2).
%C A028958 Nonnegative integers of the form x^2 + x*y + 6*y^2, discriminant -23. - _Ray Chandler_, Jul 12 2014
%C A028958 The Gram matrix is positive-definite, therefore, if w := (1 + sqrt(-23)) / 2, then |x + w*y|^2 = x^2 + x*y + 6*y^2 > 0 for all integers x and y except x = y = 0. - _Michael Somos_, Mar 28 2015
%C A028958 The theta function of the lattice with basis [1, w] is the g.f. of A028959, therefore, A028959(n) is positive if and only if n is in this sequence. - _Michael Somos_, Mar 28 2015
