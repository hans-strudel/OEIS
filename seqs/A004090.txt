%C A004090 a(n) and Fib(n) are congruent modulo 9 which implies that (a(n) mod 9) is equal to (Fib(n) mod 9) A007887(n). Thus (a(n) mod 9) is periodic with the Pisano period A001175(9) = 24. - _Hieronymus Fischer_, Jun 25 2007
%C A004090 It appears that a(n)-n stays negative for n > 5832, which explains why A020995 is finite. - _T. D. Noe_, Mar 19 2012
