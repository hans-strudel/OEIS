%N A060817 Size of the automorphism group of the alternating group A_n.
%C A060817 For all n except n=2, n=3 and n=6, Aut(A_n) is S_n i.e. a(n) = n! while Aut(A_2) = S_1, Aut(A_3) = Aut(C_3) = C_2 = S_2 and Aut(A_6) contains S_6 as a subgroup of index 2.
