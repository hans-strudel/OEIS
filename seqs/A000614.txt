%N A000614 Complemented types of Boolean functions of n variables under action of AG(n,2).
%C A000614 From Philippe Langevin's article: Let m be a positive integer. The space of Boolean functions from GF(2)^m into GF(2) is denoted by RM(k,m). This notation comes from coding theory, where it is the Reed-Muller code of order k in m variables. The affine group AG(2, m) acts on the spaces RM(k,m), and thus on RM(k,m)/RM(s,m) when s<=k. - _Jonathan Vos Post_, Feb 08 2011
