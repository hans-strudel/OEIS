%N A000545 Number of ways of n-coloring a dodecahedron.
%C A000545 More explicitly, a(n) is the number of colorings with at most n colors of the faces of a regular dodecahedron, inequivalent under the action of the rotation group of the dodecahedron. It is also the number of inequivalent colorings of the vertices of a regular icosahedron using at most n colors. - _Jos√© H. Nieto S._, Jan 19 2012
