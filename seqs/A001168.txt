%N A001168 Number of fixed polyominoes with n cells.
%C A001168 Number of rookwise connected patterns of n square cells.
%C A001168 N. Madras proved in 1999 the existence of lim_{n->oo} a(n+1)/a(n), which is the real limit growth rate of the number of polyominoes; and hence, this limit is equal to lim_{n->oo} a(n)^{1/n}, the well-known Klarner's constant. The currently best-known lower and upper bounds on this constant are 3.9801 (Barequet et al., 2006) and 4.6496 (Klarner and Rivest, 1973), respectively. But see also Knuth (2014).
