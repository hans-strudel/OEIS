%N A056650 a(n) is the greatest prime factor of a(n-1)^2+a(n-1)+1.
%C A056650 Except for the first term the initial (ten at least) terms are one more than a multiple of 6, with the result that a(n)^2+a(n)+1 is in these cases an odd multiple of 3 and appears to be 3 times the product of primes all of which are one more than a multiple of 6.
