%N A055492 Numbers n such that LCM{1, ..., n} is a minimal number.
%C A055492 Minimal numbers (A007416): let A(h) = least positive integer having exactly h divisors, let d(n) = number of divisors of n; then n is minimal if A(d(n)) = n; i.e. if n is the least positive integer having the number of divisors it has.
%C A055492 Also the numbers n such that LCM (1, ..., n) is a highly composite number (A002182). - _Matthew Vandermast_, Jul 12 2004
