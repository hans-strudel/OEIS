%N A017125 Table read by antidiagonals of Fibonacci-type sequences.
%F A017125 T(n, k) = T(n, k-1)+T(n, k-2) [with T(n, 0) = n and T(n, 1) = 1] = 2*T(n-1, k)-T(n-2, k) = Fib(k)+n*Fib(k-1) = (s^k*(1+2n/s)-t^k*(1+2n/t))/(2^k*sqrt(5)) where s = (1+sqrt(5))/2 and t = (1-sqrt(5))/2 = 1-s.
%F A017125 G.f. for n-th row: (n+x-nx)/(1-x-x^2).
