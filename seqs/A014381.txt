%N A014381 Number of connected regular graphs of degree 9 with 2n nodes.
%C A014381 Since the nontrivial 9-regular graph with the least number of vertices is K_10, there are no disconnected 9-regular graphs with less than 20 vertices. Thus for n<20 this sequence also gives the number of all 9-regular graphs on 2n vertices. - _Jason Kimberley_, Sep 25 2009
