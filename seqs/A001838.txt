%N A001838 Numbers n such that phi(n+2) = phi(n) + 2.
%C A001838 If p and p+2 are primes then p is a solution. If p and 2p+1 are both odd primes then 4p is a solution. Several numbers of the form 2^i-2 are solutions (see cross referenced sequences). Although 18 is a solution, it is not of any of these forms.
%C A001838 Twice Mersenne primes (cf. A000668) are also solutions. - _Vladeta Jovovic_, Feb 14 2002
