%N A052249 Triangle T(n,k) (n >= 1, k >= 1) giving dimension of bigrading of Connes-Moscovici noncocommutative algebra.
%C A052249 With rows reversed, T(n,k) appears to be the number of partitions of n with k big parts, where a big part is a part >=2 (0<=k<=n/2). For example, with n=4, the 3 partitions 4, 31, 211 each have one big part. [David Callan, Aug 23 2011]
