%N A061806 Numbers n such that the iterative cycle: n -> sum of digits of n^2 has only three distinct elements.
%e A061806 4 -> 1+6 = 7 -> 4+9 = 13 -> 1+6+9 = 16 -> 2+5+6 = 13, thus {7,13,16} are the only distinct elements of the iterative cycle of 4.
