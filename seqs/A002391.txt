%F A002391 log(3) = Sum {k >= 0} 1/((2*k + 1)*4^k).
%F A002391 Define a pair of integer sequences A(n) = 4^n*(2*n + 1)!/n! and B(n) = A(n)*sum {k = 0..n} (1/((2*k + 1)*4^k). Both sequences satisfy the same second order recurrence equation u(n) = (20*n + 6)*u(n-1) - 16*(2*n - 1)^2*u(n-2). From this observation we obtain the continued fraction expansion log(3) = 1 + 2/(24 - 16*3^2/(46 - 16*5^2/(66 - ... - 16*(2*n - 1)^2/((20*n + 6) - ... )))). Cf. A002162, A073000 and A105531 for similar expansions.
