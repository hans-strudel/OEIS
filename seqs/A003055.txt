%N A003055 Number of connected graphs, up to homeomorphism, that can be drawn in the plane using unit-length edges.
%C A003055 K_4 can't be so drawn even though it is planar. Although a square with a tail of length 1 and a triangle with a tail of length 2 are nonisomorphic graphs with five edges, they are homeomorphic as topological spaces.
