%N A061061 Maximal number of 132 patterns in a permutation of 1,2,...,n.
%C A061061 a(n) = A216499(n) - (n choose 3). lim_{n --> infinity} a(n) / n^3 = (2 sqrt(3) - 3) / 6 = 0.077350... a(n) / n^3 < (2 sqrt(3) - 3) / 6 = 0.077350... for all n > 0. [Chao et al. (2012)]. - _Jesper Jansson_, Sep 10 2012
