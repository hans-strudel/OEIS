%N A014382 Number of connected regular graphs of degree 10 with n nodes.
%C A014382 Since the nontrivial 10-regular graph with the least number of vertices is K_11, there are no disconnected 10-regular graphs with less than 22 vertices. Thus for n<22 this sequence also counts the number of all 10-regular graphs on n vertices. - _Jason Kimberley_, Sep 25 2009
