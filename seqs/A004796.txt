%N A004796 n-th positive integer k such that if 2 <= j < k then the fractional part of the k-th partial sum of harmonic series is < the fractional part of the j-th partial sum of the harmonic series.
%C A004796 n such that H(n) sets a new record for being a tiny bit greater than an integer, where H(n)=sum_{k=1..n} 1/k. For proofs that H(n) is non-integral and almost always a non-terminating decimal see Havil reference.
%C A004796 Assuming that H(n) ~= log(n) + gamma + 1/(2n), the next several entries should be 2012783315, 5471312310 and 40427833596; 14872568831 and 109894245429 are not included. - _Robert G. Wilson v_
