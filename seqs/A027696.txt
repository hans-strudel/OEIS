%N A027696 Nonparametric solutions to problem in A027669: n such that for some m, the sum of the first m n-gonal numbers is again an n-gonal number.
%F A027696 The parametric solution: if n=2 mod 3, the sum of the first (n^2-4*n-2)/3 n-gonal numbers is the ((n^3-6*n^2+3*n+19)/9)-th n-gonal number.
