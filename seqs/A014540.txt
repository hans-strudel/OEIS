%N A014540 Rectilinear crossing number of complete graph on n nodes.
%C A014540 The values a(19) and a(21) were obtained by Aichholzer et al. in 2006. The value a(18) is claimed by the Rectilinear Crossing Number project after months of distributed computing. This was confirmed by Abrego et al., they also found the values a(20) and a(22) to a(27). The next unknown entry, a(28), is either 7233 or 7234. - Bernardo M. Abrego (bernardo.abrego(AT)csun.edu), May 05 2008
