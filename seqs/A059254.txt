%N A059254 Size of maximal subset of the Sobol sequence (projection of a 0/1-cube with the projection vectors b_i=(i,rev(i)), where rev(i) is the value of the reversed binary representation of i).
%C A059254 Also currently the maximal size of the boundary length of a projection of a 0/1-polytope into the plane. The values have been found by a dynamic programming algorithm for dimensions up to 13. For higher values the memory requirements of the algorithm exceed current capacities.
%D A059254 Kortenkamp, U.H.; Richter-Gebert, J.; Sarangarajan, A.; Ziegler, G.M. Extremal properties of $0/1$-polytopes. (English) Discrete Comput. Geom. 17, No.4, 439-448 (1997).
