%N A028877 Primes of form n^2 - 5.
%C A028877 These numbers are prime in Z but not in Z[sqrt(5)] nor in Z[phi] (where phi is the golden ratio), since (n - sqrt(5))(n + sqrt(5)) = ((n + 1) - 2phi)((n - 1) + 2phi) = n^2 - 5. - _Alonso del Arte_, Aug 27 2013
