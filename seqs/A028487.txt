%N A028487 Number of perfect matchings in graph C_{16} X P_{n}.
%C A028487 For even values of m the order of the recurrence relation for the number of perfect matchings in graph C_{m} X P_{n} does not exceed 2^odd(m/2)*3^floor(m/4) where odd(k)=1 for odd k and 0 otherwise. In general, as for this sequence, the estimate is accurate. - _Sergey Perepechko_, Sep 25 2015
