%N A005670 Mrs. Perkins's quilt: smallest coprime dissection of n X n square.
%C A005670 The problem is to dissect an n X n square into smaller integer squares, the GCD of whose sides is 1, using the smallest number of squares. The GCD condition excludes dissecting a 6 X 6 into four 3 X 3 squares.
%C A005670 The name "Mrs Perkins's Quilt" comes from a problem in one of Dudeney's books, wherein he gives the answer for n = 13. I gave the answers for low n and an upper bound of order n^(1/3) for general n, which Trustrum improved to order log(n). There's an obvious logarithmic lower bound. - _J. H. Conway_, Oct 11 2003
%C A005670 All entries shown are known to be correct - see Wynn, 2013. - _N. J. A. Sloane_, Nov 29 2013
