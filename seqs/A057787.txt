%N A057787 Number of polyarcs with n cells.
%C A057787 Draw a quarter circle with radius one, centered at the corner of a unit square. It divides the square into two pieces. Call these pieces monarcs. Polyarcs are the figures created by joining monarcs edge-to-edge. - _Henri Picciotto_, Jan 04 2015
%C A057787 Henri Picciotto invented and named the polyarcs in the late 1980’s. They were first published in World Games Review, Michael Keller’s zine. Brendan Owen found and counted the polyarcs to n = 9. - _N. J. A. Sloane_, Mar 29 2015
