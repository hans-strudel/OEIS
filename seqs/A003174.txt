%S A003174 2,3,5,6,7,11,13,17,19,21,29,33,37,41,57,73
%N A003174 Positive integers D such that Q[sqrt(D)] is a quadratic field which is norm-Euclidean.
%C A003174 These integers yield norm-Euclidean real quadratic fields. There are other positive integers, e.g., D=14 or D=69, for which Q[sqrt(D)] is Euclidean, but for an Euclidean function different from the field norm.
%C A003174 For further references see sequence A048981 which also lists negative D corresponding to (complex) norm-Euclidean fields. - _M. F. Hasler_, Jan 26 2014
