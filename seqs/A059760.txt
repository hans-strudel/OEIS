%N A059760 a(n) is the number of edges (one-dimensional faces) in the convex polytope of real n X n doubly stochastic matrices. The vertices are the n! permutation matrices. If A(p1) and A(p2) are two permutation matrices corresponding to permutations p1 and p2 the closed interval between these two matrices forms an edge of the polytope iff the permutation p1*(p2^-1) is a cycle, i.e. its cycle decomposition in the symmetric group S_n contains exactly one nontrivial cycle.
%H A059760 Alois P. Heinz, <a href="/A059760/b059760.txt">Table of n, a(n) for n = 0..250</a>
%F A059760 a(n) = 1/2* n! * Sum_{k=2...n} C(n,k)*(k-1)!.
%e A059760 a(3) = 15 because there are 3! = 6 vertices and C(6,2) intervals and in this case all are edges so a(3) = C(6,2) = 15.
