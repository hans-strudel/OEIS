%F A050438 Let C(n) be the n-th composite number, with C(1)=4. Then these are numbers C(C(C(C(n)))).
%e A050438 C(C(C(C(8)))) = C(C(C(15))) = C(C(25)) = C(38) = 55. So 55 is in the sequence.
%p A050438 C := remove(isprime,[$4..1000]): seq(C[C[C[C[n]]]],n=1..100);
