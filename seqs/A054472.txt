%N A054472 Number of ways to color faces of a icosahedron using at most n colors.
%C A054472 More explicitly, a(n) is the number of colorings with at most n colors of the faces of a regular icosahedron, inequivalent under the action of the rotation group of the icosahedron. It is also the number of inequivalent colorings of the vertices of a regular dodecahedron using at most n colors. - _Jos√© H. Nieto S._, Jan 19 2012
