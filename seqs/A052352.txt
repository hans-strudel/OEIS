%N A052352 First primes of A031924 (lesser of 6-twins, geminor 6 primes) with increasing distance to the next 6-twin.
%C A052352 1. The increment of distance of 6-twins (A053321) is 2 (not 6), the smallest distance (A052380) is 6.
%C A052352 2. The middle gap 2n-2 may include primes, e.g. n=10,a(10)=653 and between 659 and 659+2*10-2=677 two primes occur.
%F A052352 a(n)=p yields a p quadruple [p, p+6, p+2n+4, p+2n+4+6] and d- pattern [6, 2n-2, 6].
%e A052352 For n=1,2,3 the quadruples are [47,53,53,59] (a triple), [23,29,31,37], [73,79,83,89] with 53-47=6,31-23=8 and 83-73=10 twin- distances.
