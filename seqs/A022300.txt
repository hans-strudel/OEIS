%C A022300 It appears that various properties and unsolved problems associated with the Oldenburger-Kolakoski sequence, A000002, apply also to A022300.
%H A022300 Clark Kimberling, <a href="/A022300/b022300.txt">Table of n, a(n) for n = 1..20000</a>
