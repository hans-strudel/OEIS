%N A061300 Least number such that number of divisors is n!.
%C A061300 a(n) = A037019(n!) for all n <= 12 except 4. I conjecture that this remains true for all larger n, i.e. 4! is the only "exceptional" factorial (see A037019). - _David Wasserman_, Jun 13 2002
%C A061300 Alternate definition: smallest number with same number of divisors as A006939 - _J. Lowell_, May 20 2008
%F A061300 A005179(n!)=a(n), for example A005179(120)=55440
%F A061300 a(n)=Min{x| A000005[x]=n!}, for example A000005(55440)=120 and 55440 is minimal.
%e A061300 a(3) = 12 and tau(12) = 6 = 3!
