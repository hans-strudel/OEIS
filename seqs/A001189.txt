%F A001189 a(n) = b(n, 2), where b(n, d)=Sum_{k=1..n} (n-1)!/(n-k)! * Sum_{l:lcm{k, l}=d} b(n-k, l), b(0, 1)=1 is the number of degree-n permutations of order exactly d.
