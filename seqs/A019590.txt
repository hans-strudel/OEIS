%N A019590 Fermat's Last Theorem: a(n) = 1 if x^n+y^n=z^n has a nontrivial solution in integers, otherwise a(n) = 0.
%C A019590 a(n) is the Hankel transform of A000045(n), n>=1 (Fibonacci numbers). See A055879 for the definition of Hankel transform. - _Wolfdieter Lang_, Jan 23 2007
%C A019590 1, -1, 0, 0, 0, ... is the convolutional inverse of the all-ones sequence. - _Tanya Khovanova_, Jun 29 2007
%C A019590 Also parity of the Euler totient function A000010. - _Omar E. Pol_, Jan 15 2012
