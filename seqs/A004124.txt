%F A004124 Sign(a(n)) = (-1)^(phi(n)*(phi(n)-1)/2). Magnitude: For prime p, a(p) = p^(p-2). For n = p^e, a prime power, a(n) = p^(((p-1)e-1) p^(e-1)). For n = prod p_i^e_i, a product of prime powers, a(n) = prod a(p_i^e_i)^phi(n/p_i^e_i).
