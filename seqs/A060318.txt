%N A060318 Powers of 3 in the odd Catalan numbers cat[2^n-1].
%C A060318 Conjecture: all odd Catalan numbers have smallest factor 3, except cat[3] has smallest divisor 5 and cat[31] and cat[255] have smallest divisor 7 (checked up to cat[ -1+2^2048 ]).
%e A060318 a(5)=0 because 2^5-1= 31 and cat[31]= 7.11.17.19.37.41.43.47.53.59.61 so the power of 3 is zero.
