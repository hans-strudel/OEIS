%N A058314 Sum of terms in simple continued fraction for n!!/(n-1)!!, where n!! is a double factorial, n(n-2)(n-4)....
%e A058314 4!!/3!! = 2*4/(1*3) = 8/3 = 2 + 1/(1 + 1/2), so the 4th term is 2 + 1 + 2 = 5.
