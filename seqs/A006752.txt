%N A006752 Decimal expansion of Catalan's constant 1 - 1/9 + 1/25 - 1/49 + 1/81 - ...
%C A006752 With the k-th appended term being 2*3*...*(2+k-2)*2^k*(2^k-1)*Bern(k) / (2*k!*(J^(k+2-1))). Bern(k) is a Bernoulli number and J is a large number of the form 4n + 1. This is from "An Atlas Of Functions" by Spanier, J. and Oldham, K. B. 1987, equation 3:3:7. - _Harry J. Smith_, May 07 2009
