%C A039943 Following after I. Ja. Tanatar (Moscow), one can immediately easily prove that, for a given x, there exists an iteration of the considered map f(x) which equals 1 or 89. Indeed, it is easy to see that if x has not less than 3 digits, then f(x)<x. Therefore, there exists an iteration of f with not more than 2 digits. For two-digit numbers the property is verified directly. See Kordemsky. - _Vladimir Shevelev_, May 06 2013
