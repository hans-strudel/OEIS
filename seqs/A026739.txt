%N A026739 Number of subgroups L of Z^n with the property that for every a in Z^n there exists precisely one b in L with d(a,b) <= 1. Here d denotes Euclidean distance.
%F A026739 a(n) = 2^n*n!*sum(1/|Aut(G)|), where the sum is over all isomorphism classes of Abelian groups of order 2*n+1.
