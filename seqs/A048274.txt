%N A048274 Let G be the graph with n vertices, the i-th vertex consisting of all multiples of i <= n, where edges indicate that the vertices intersect; a(n) = |n-(number of edges of G)|.
%e A048274 For n=6 the vertices are {1,2,3,4,5,6},{2,4,6},{3,6},{4},{5},{6}. There are 9 = 5+3+1 edges and so a(6) = |6-9| = 3.
