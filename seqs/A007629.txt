%N A007629 Repfigit (REPetitive FIbonacci-like diGIT) numbers (or Keith numbers).
%C A007629 Numbers n>9 with following property: form a sequence b(i) whose initial terms are the t digits of n, later terms given by rule that b(i) = sum of t previous terms; then n itself appears in the sequence.
