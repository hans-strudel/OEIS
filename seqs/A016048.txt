%N A016048 Least k such that (2*p_n)*k + 1 | Mersenne(p_n), p_n = n-th prime, n >= 2.
%C A016048 M(p_n) = 2^p_n - 1 = (2*p_n)*j + 1 = [(2*p_n)*k_1 + 1] ... [(2*p_n)*k_i + 1], n >= 2 (i.e. odd prime p_n), i >= 1. Then k = Min(k_1, ..., k_i).
