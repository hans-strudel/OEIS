%N A007614 All values attained by the phi(n) function, in ascending order.
%C A007614 Write down phi(1), phi(2), phi(3), ..., then sort this list. Of course the list before sorting is simply sequence A000010.
%C A007614 To ensure that all terms are found, the values of phi(n) should be computed for all n up to a primorial p# -- which are the local minima of the phi function. Selecting and sorting the values of phi(n) <= phi(p#) produces the terms of this sequence. - T. D. Noe, Mar 22 2011
