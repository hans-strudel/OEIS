%N A002489 n^(n^2), or (n^n)^n.
%C A002489 The number of closed binary operations on a set of order n. Labeled groupoids.
%C A002489 The values of "googol" in base N: "10^100" in base 2 is 2^4=16; "10^100" in base 3 is 3^9=19683, etc. This is N^^3 by the "lower-valued" (left-associative) definition of the hyper4 or tetration operator (see Munafo webpage). - _Robert Munafo_, Jan 25 2010
%C A002489 n^(n^k) = (((n^n)^n)^...)^n, with k+1 n's, k >= 0. - _Daniel Forgues_, May 18 2013
