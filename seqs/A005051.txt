%C A005051 For n>=3, a(n-3) is equal to the number of functions f:{1,2,...,n}->{1,2,3} such that for fixed, different x_1, x_2, x_3 in {1,2,...,n} and fixed y_1, y_2, y_3 in {1,2,3} we have f(x_i)<>y_i, (i=1,2,3). - _Milan Janjic_, May 13 2007
