%N A048987 Number of possible chess games at the end of the n-th ply.
%C A048987 Does not include games which end in fewer than n plies.
%C A048987 According to the laws of chess, the "50-move rule" and "draw by 3-fold repetition" do not prevent infinite games because they require an appeal by one of the players, but the "75-move rule" introduced on Jul 01 2014 is automatic and makes chess finite. - _François Labelle_, Mar 30 2015
