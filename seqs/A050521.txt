%N A050521 Number of factorizations of (n,2*n) into pairs (k,l).
%C A050521 Pairs (k,l) must satisfy 0<k, 0<l; if k=1 then l=1. Definition of "*": (a,b)*(x,y)=(a*x,b*y); unit is (1,1).
%C A050521 Factorization is up to ordering of pairs, thus (2,2)*(5,10) is the same as (5,10)*(2,2). Factorizations may involve an arbitrary number of pairs, (2,2)*(3,4)*(5,6)=(30,48). - _Sean A. Irvine_, Mar 21 2013
%H A050521 Giovanni Resta, <a href="/A050521/b050521.txt">Table of n, a(n) for n = 1..1000</a>
%e A050521 (10,20) = (2,1)*(5,20) = (2,20)*(5*1) = (2,2)*(5,10) = (2,10)*(5,2) = (2,4)*(5,5) = (2,5)*(5,4); so a(10) = 7.
