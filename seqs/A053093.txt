%N A053093 Smallest composite which when sum of prime factors is repeatedly subtracted reaches a prime after n iterations.
%e A053093 a(5)=104. Iteration 1: 104=2*2*2*13 and sum of factors is 19; 104-19=85. Iteration 2: 85=5*17; 85-22=63. Iterations 3,4,5 produce 63-13=50, 50-12=38 and 38-21=17, a prime.
