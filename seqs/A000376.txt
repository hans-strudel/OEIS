%N A000376 Topswops (2): start by shuffling n cards labeled 1..n. If top card is m, reverse order of top m cards. Repeat until 1 gets to top, then stop. Suppose the whole deck is now sorted (if not, discard this case). a(n) is the maximal number of steps before 1 got to the top.
%C A000376 See also A000375, which is the main entry for this problem.
