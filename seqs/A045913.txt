%N A045913 Kaprekar numbers, allowing powers of 10: n such that n=q+r and n^2=q*10^m+r, for some m >= 1, q>=0 and 0<=r<10^m. Here q and r must both be m-digit numbers.
%C A045913 Powers of 10 were excluded in Kaprekar's original definition (A006886).
