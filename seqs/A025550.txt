%N A025550 a(n) = ( 1/1 + 1/3 + 1/5 + ... + 1/(2*n-1) )*LCM(1, 3, 5, ..., 2*n-1).
%C A025550 Or, numerator of 1/1 + 1/3 + ... + 1/(2n-1) up to a(38).
%C A025550 Following similar remark by _T. D. Noe_ in A025547, this coincides with f(n) = numerator of 1+1/3+1/5+1/7+...+1/(2n-1) iff n <= 38. But a(39) = 18048708369314455836683437302413, f(39)=1640791669937677803334857936583. Note that f(n)=numerator(digamma(n+1/2)/2+log(2)+euler_gamma/2). - _Paul Barry_, Aug 19 2005
