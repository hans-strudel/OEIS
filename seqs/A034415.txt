%N A034415 Second term in extremal weight enumerator of doubly-even binary self-dual code of length 24n.
%C A034415 The terms become negative at n=154 and so certainly by that point the extremal codes do not exist (see references).
%C A034415 Up to n = 250 the terms steadily increase in magnitude, but their sign changes from positive to negative at n = 154.
