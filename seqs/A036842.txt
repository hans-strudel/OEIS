%N A036842 Smallest leg in right triangle with relatively prime sides and hypotenuse 5^n.
%C A036842 If (3+4i)^n = r+si, then a(n) is the minimum of |r| and |s|.
%H A036842 Zak Seidov <a href="http://web.archive.org/web/20090729173719/http://geocities.com/zseidov/">Triggeom</a>, see item 29.
