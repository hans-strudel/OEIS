%N A048870 Triangle of coefficients of certain Sheffer-polynomials.
%C A048870 s(n,x) := sum(a(n,m)*x^m,m=0..n) are monic polynomials satisfying s(n,x+y) = sum(binomial(n,k)*s(k,x)*p(n-k,y),k=0..n), with polynomials p(n,x)=sum(A048786(n,m)*x^m, m=1..n) (row polynomials of triangle A048786) and p(0,x)=1. In the umbral calculus (see reference) the s(n,x) are called Sheffer polynomials for(c(t/(1+4*t)),t/(1+4*t)), where c(x) = g.f. for Catalan numbers A000108. a(n,0) = A001761(n-2) = n!*A000108(n).
%D A048870 S. Roman, The Umbral Calculus, Academic Press, New York, 1984.
%F A048870 a(n, m) = (n!/m!)*A046527(n, m) = (n!/m!)*binomial(n, m-1)*(4^(n-m+1)-binomial(2*n, n)/binomial(2*(m-1), m-1))/2, n >= m >= 0, a(n, m) := 0, n<m.
