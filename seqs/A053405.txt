%N A053405 Definition: A kara B = C, where C is the least nonnegative integer such that: C * B >= A and C * (B-1) < A. Sequence gives smallest a such that n kara a is undefined.
%H A053405 Reinhard Zumkeller, <a href="/A053405/b053405.txt">Table of n, a(n) for n = 4..10000</a>
%e A053405 For instance, 7 kara 4 = 2 because 2 * 4 > 7 and 2 * 3 < 7. 7 kara 5 is undefined: 7 kara 5 != 2 because 2 * (5-1) > 7 and 7 kara 5 != 1 because 1 * 5 < 7. 4 kara 3 is undefined: 4 kara 2 = 2; 4 kara 4 = 1.
