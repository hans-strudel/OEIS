%N A047239 Numbers that are congruent to {1, 2} mod 6.
%C A047239 If a(n) is the n-th Towers of Hanoi move, the smallest disc (#1) is on peg C. If n == (3,4) mod 6, disc #1 is on peg B; and if n == (0,5) disc #1 is on peg A. Disc #1 moves every 1,3,5,7,...th move in a rotational cycle CBACBACBA such that it's on peg C the first TOH move and stays there for the 2nd move (in which case disc #2 moves). Therefore disc #1 is on peg C in moves (1, 2, 7, 8, 13,...). - _Gary W. Adamson_, Jun 22 2012
