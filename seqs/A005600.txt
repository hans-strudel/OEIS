%N A005600 Current estimate of decimal expansion of reciprocal of fine-structure constant alpha.
%C A005600 The estimate is continually changing.
%C A005600 The fine-structure constant actually isn't one, it depends on the energy at which it is measured: Its value is known to approach 1/128 at interaction energies above 80 GeV. For other considerations, e.g., whether the fine-structure constant's value differs by location and over time, see the Wikipedia article or other links. - _M. F. Hasler_, Oct 30 2013
