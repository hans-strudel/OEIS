%N A005230 Stern's sequence: a(1) = 1, a(n+1) is the sum of the m preceding terms, where m*(m-1)/2 < n <= m*(m+1)/2 or equivalently m = ceil((sqrt(8*n+1)-1)/2) = A002024(n).
