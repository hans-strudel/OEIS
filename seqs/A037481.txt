%N A037481 Base-4 digits are, in order, the first n terms of the periodic sequence with initial period 1,2.

%C A037481 These partitions are the only fixed points of "Bulgarian Solitaire" operation (see Gardner reference or Wikipedia page), and thus the terms of this sequence give the fixed points for A226062 which implements that operation (using the same encoding for partitions). This also implies that these partitions are the roots of the game trees constructed for decks consisting of 1+2+3+...+k cards. See A227451 for the encoding of the corresponding tops of the main trunks of the same trees.
