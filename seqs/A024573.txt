%N A024573 a(n) = floor(1/frac(n*e)).
%C A024573 The sequence is well defined, since frac(n*e)>0 for n>0.
%C A024573 Let b(n,m) = |{a(k)| 1<=k<=n, a(k)>=m}| be the number of the first n terms which are >= m >= 1.
%C A024573 Then, lim b(n,m)/n = 1/m for n-->oo since frac(n*e) is uniformly distributed. - _Hieronymus Fischer_, Apr 15 2012
