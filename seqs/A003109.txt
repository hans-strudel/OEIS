%N A003109 a(n) = number of special even permutations of 2*n+1.
%C A003109 a(n) is the number of even permutations, pi(0),...,pi(2*n), of 0,...,2*n such that pi(0) = 0 and p(k) = k + pi(k) (mod 2*n+1), k=0,...,2*n is also a permutation of 0,...,2*n. - _Sean A. Irvine_, Jan 30 2015
