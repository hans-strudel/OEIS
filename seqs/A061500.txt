%N A061500 Integer part of arithmetic mean of distinct numbers that can be formed from the nonzero digits of n.
%D A061500 Amarnath Murthy, An Interesting result in Combinatorics, Mathematics and Informatics Quarterly, 3/99, vol. 9, Bulgaria.
%e A061500 a(10) = floor(1+10)/2 =5. a(12) = 9: the distinct numbers formed by the digits of 12 are 1, 2, 12, 21. The arithmetic mean of these numbers is 36/4 = 9.
