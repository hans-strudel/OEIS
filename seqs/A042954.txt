%N A042954 The sequence d when b=[ 1,1,0,1,1,.. ].
%C A042954 Map a binary sequence b=[ b_1,... ] to a binary sequence c=[ c_1,... ] so that C=1/Product((1-x^i)^c_i == 1+Sum b_i*x^i mod 2.
%C A042954 This produces 2 new sequences: d={i:c_i=1} and e=[ 1,e_1,... ] where C=1+Sum e_i*x^i.
