%N A059339 A+B=C for 0<=A, 0<=B, A<=B; if n is a C-almost prime then a(n) = number of decompositions of n into sum of an A-almost prime and a B-almost prime.
%C A059339 E.g. n=10, 10 is 2-almost prime (semiprime). Therefore C=2. 10=1+9(A=0,B=2); 10=7+3(A=1,B=1); 10=5+5(A=1,B=1), so a(10)=3.
