%N A057204 Primes congruent to 1 mod 6 generated recursively. Initial prime is 7. The next term is p(n) = Min {p is prime; p divides 4Q^2+3; Mod[p,6]=1}, where Q is the product of previous entries of the sequence.
%C A057204 4Q^2+3 always has a prime divisor congruent to 1 modulo 6.
%C A057204 If we start with the empty product Q=1 then it is not necessary to specify the initial prime. - _Jens Kruse Andersen_, Jun 30 2014

%e A057204 a(4)=487 is the smallest prime divisor of 4QQ+3=10812186007, congruent to 1 mod 6, where Q=7.199.7761799.

%t A057204     ];
