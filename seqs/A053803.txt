%N A053803 Numbers where the difference of consecutive cubes is "close" to another cube: let A = x^3 - (x-1)^3, sequence is the x's where A - int(A^(1/3))^3 < int(x^(1/2))^3.
%e A053803 a(2)=7 because A = 7^3-6^3 = 127 and the condition 'A - int(A^(1/3))^3 < int(x^(1/2))^3' simplifies to '127 - 5^3 < 2^3' which is true.
