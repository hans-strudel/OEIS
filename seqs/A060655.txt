%N A060655 Pack n integer sided rectangles into the smallest possible square so that no sides of the rectangle are the same. Sequence gives the side of the smallest square.
%C A060655 Those terms that are > ceil(sqrt(n(n+1)(2n+1)/3)) are only the best currently known solutions.
