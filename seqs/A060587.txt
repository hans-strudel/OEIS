%C A060587 Write n in base 3, then (working from left to right) if the k-th digit of n is equal to the digit to the left of it then this is the k-th digit of a(n), otherwise the k-th digit of a(n) is the element of {0,1,2} which has not just been compared, then read result as a base 3 number.
