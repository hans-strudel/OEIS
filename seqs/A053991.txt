%F A053991 {1/((n^2-1)k+1){{n^2 k} choose {k}} modulo {n^2} | k\in N}
%e A053991 a(2)=3 since there are 3 residues of 1/((2^2-1)n+1){{2^2 n} choose {n}} modulo {2^2}, namely {0,1,2}.
