%N A054767 Period of the sequence of Bell numbers A000110 (mod n).
%C A054767 For p prime, a(p) divides (p^p-1)/(p-1) = A023037(p), with equality at least for p up to 19.
%C A054767 Wagstaff shows that N(p) = (p^p-1)/(p-1) is the period for all primes p < 102 and for primes p = 113, 163, 167 and 173. For p = 7547, N(p) is a probable prime, which means that this p may have the maximum possible period N(p) also. See A088790. [From _T. D. Noe_, Dec 17 2008]
