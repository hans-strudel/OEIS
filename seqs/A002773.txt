%N A002773 Number of nonisomorphic simple matroids (or geometries) with n points.
%C A002773 Counts simple matroids, which necessarily cannot have loops or parallel elements. Bansal (2012) shows that  log log a(n) <= n - (3/2)log n +  2 log log n+O(1), which matches Knuth's lower bound up to second order terms. [Jonathan Vos Post, Jun 27 2012]
