%F A000198 a(3^k) = 3^((3^k - 1)/2), a(5*3^k) = 5*3^((5*3^k - 5)/2), a(7*3^k) = 7*3^((7*3^k - 5)/2), and, for all other n, a(n) = max (a(i)a(n-i)) where the max is taken over 1 <= i <= n-1 (from Alspach and Berggren (1973) Theorem 4).
%F A000198 a(3r) = (3^r)a(r), a(n) = a(n-1) for n = 1, 2 or 4 mod 9, a(9k+8) = max(a(9k+7), a(5)a(9k+3)), a(9k+5) = max(a(2)a(9k+3), a(5)a(9k), a(7)a(9k-2)), a(9k+7) = a(7)a(9k) (from Alspach and Berggren (1973) Theorem 5).
