%N A000792 a(n) = max{ (n-i)*a(i) : i<n}; a(0) = 1.
%C A000792 Numbers of the form 3^n, 2*3^n, 4*3^n with a(0) = 1 prepended.
%C A000792 If a set of positive numbers has sum n, this is the largest value of their product.
%C A000792 In other words, maximum of products of partitions of n: maximal value of Product k_i for any way of writing n = Sum k_i. To find the answer, take as many of the k_i's as possible to be 3 and then use one or two 2's (see formula lines below).
